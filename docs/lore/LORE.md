## 🌍 The World: Gea Prime

The world of **Gea Prime** is a living planet — its crust is threaded with glowing channels called **The Veins**. These Veins are simultaneously:

- **Mineral veins** — carrying precious ores, gems, and the legendary **Veinstone**
- **Life veins** — carrying genetic essence from the planet's core, the source of all life
- **Memory veins** — carrying the accumulated wisdom of every bloodline that has ever walked the surface

Gea Prime is a harsh world. Surface kingdoms fight for control of the richest Vein deposits. Underground, the **Deep Guilds** have mastered the art of Veinwalking — the ability to sense, mine, and channel Vein energy.

The currency of this world is **$VEIN** — raw Vein energy extracted from the planet's depths. Those who control the Veins control everything.

## 🧬 The Bloodlines (Character Classes)

Every character in VeinLegends belongs to one of six ancient Bloodlines, each descended from the original **Prime Veinwalkers** who first discovered how to channel Vein energy.

### ⛏️ The Delvers (Miners)

> *"We don't seek the Vein. The Vein seeks us."*

The oldest bloodline. The Delvers can HEAR the Veins — a low hum that guides them to the richest deposits. They are the backbone of the Vein economy, the first to arrive at every new Vein discovery.

**Signature:** Vein Sense — passive mining boost
**Home:** The Deep Warrens beneath the Rustfang Mountains

### ⚔️ The Ironblood (Warriors)

> *"Our blood IS the Vein."*

Descended from a Prime Veinwalker who infused his own blood with molten Veinstone. The Ironblood are feared gladiators who fight with Vein-enhanced strength. Their skin shimmers with metallic veins after each battle.

**Signature:** First Strike — their blood boils faster than any enemy
**Home:** The Crucible, a floating colosseum suspended over an active Vein rift

### 🔥 The Forgeborn (Forger)

> *"Some are born of flesh. We are born of fire and Vein."*

Not a bloodline by birth — the Forgeborn are CHOSEN. When a Veinwalker masters the art of channeling Vein energy through flame, they undergo the Ritual of Reforging: they step into a Vein-forge and emerge transformed — their blood replaced with liquid fire.

**Signature:** Vein Burn — attacks set enemies ablaze with Vein-fire
**Home:** The Ember Anvil, a forge-city built inside a dormant volcano

### 🗡️ The Shadowveins (Scouts)

> *"We move where the Vein is thinnest... and strike where it's weakest."*

Outcasts from every other bloodline, the Shadowveins learned to survive in the Vein-silent zones — the dead spaces between rich deposits. They move unseen, their bloodlines adapted to darkness and silence.

**Signature:** Vein Stealth — can sense enemy Vein signatures before they're visible
**Home:** The Grey Expanse, a labyrinth of abandoned mine tunnels

### 🛡️ The Stonewardens (Guardians)

> *"The Vein does not protect itself. WE do."*

Guardians of the **Heartvein** — the largest known Vein deposit, located at the planet's core. The Stonewardens have stood watch for 10,000 years, their bloodlines interwoven with the very rock they protect.

**Signature:** Vein Fortify — channel surrounding rock into impenetrable armor
**Home:** The Bastion, a fortress carved into the planet's mantle

### 🧪 The Veinbenders (Alchemists)

> *"The Vein is not a resource. It's a language. And we are the only ones fluent."*

Mad scientists and heretic philosophers. The Veinbenders believe the Veins are a CONSCIOUS entity — a planetary intelligence — and that by mixing Vein extracts with alchemical compounds, they can communicate with it.

**Signature:** Vein Transmutation — steal enemy Vein energy and convert it
**Home:** The Synapse Spire, a crystal tower growing from a Vein intersection

## ⚔️ The Great Sundering (Backstory)

500 years ago, Gea Prime was united under the **Vein Concord** — a pact between all six Bloodlines to share the Veins equally.

Then came **The Sundering**.

An unknown catastrophe shattered the Heartvein, splitting the planet's Vein network into isolated fragments. The Bloodlines blamed each other. The Concord collapsed. The **Bloodline Wars** began — 300 years of endless conflict.

Now, in the **Age of Reclamation**, a new generation of Veinwalkers is rising. They understand what their ancestors forgot: the Veins are dying. The fragments are shrinking. Without intervention, Gea Prime will become a dead world within a century.

The only solution? **Merge the Bloodlines.**

By breeding across Bloodline boundaries — combining Delver endurance with Ironblood ferocity, Forgeborn fire with Shadowvein stealth — a new hybrid generation can access multiple Vein frequencies simultaneously. Only these hybrids can navigate the fragmented Vein network and re-connect the Heartvein.

This is the purpose of the **Veinblood Trials**: a planetary competition where the strongest hybrids battle, breed, and evolve in pursuit of the ultimate Veinwalker — the **Prime Hybrid** who can heal the Sundering.

## 🎮 The Player's Role

You are a **Veinwalker** arriving at the [Hub City name TBD] — a neutral settlement built on one of the few remaining stable Vein intersections.

Your journey:

1. **Mine the Veins** — Extract $VEIN from the fragment you control. Build your resource base.

2. **Forge Your First Character** — Channel Vein energy into a living being. Choose your starting Bloodline.

3. **Breed Your Bloodline** — Find another Veinwalker. Combine your characters' genetic Vein signatures. Create hybrids with unprecedented power.

4. **Enter the Veinblood Trials (PvP)** — Battle other Veinwalkers in the arena. Each victory brings you closer to the Prime Hybrid title.

5. **Unlock Ancient Vein Memories** — As you progress, fragments of pre-Sundering knowledge are recovered from the Veins' genetic memory.

6. **Heal the Sundering** — The ultimate endgame: assemble a team of hybrids powerful enough to re-enter the Heartvein and restore Gea Prime.

## 💀 The Threat: The Hollowed

Not everyone wants the Veins restored.

The **Hollowed** are Veinwalkers who tried to merge too many Bloodlines too quickly. The genetic overload burned out their Vein receptors — leaving them as soulless husks who can no longer sense Vein energy.

Now they roam the Vein-silent zones, draining $VEIN from any living being they encounter. They are the dark mirror of the player: what every Veinwalker could become if they grow too greedy, too fast.

The Hollowed serve the **Veinsbane**, an ancient entity that was imprisoned inside the Heartvein at the moment of the Sundering. As players breed stronger hybrids, the Veinsbane grows stronger too — feeding on the energy released by every new bloodline combination.

The final battle is not just against other players. It's against the Veinsbane itself.

## 🏛️ Factions

### The Restoration Concord
- **Goal:** Heal the Sundering, reunite the Bloodlines
- **Founded by:** Elder Veinwalkers who remember the old Concord
- **Questline:** Exploration, diplomacy, Vein research

### The Deep Extraction Guild
- **Goal:** Extract maximum $VEIN before the Veins die
- **Founded by:** The Delver leadership during the Bloodline Wars
- **Questline:** Resource optimization, territorial control, market dominance

### The Veinsbane Cult
- **Goal:** Prevent the Sundering from being healed — keep the Veinsbane imprisoned
- **Founded by:** A rogue Veinbender who glimpsed the Veinsbane's true power
- **Questline:** Espionage, sabotage, forbidden knowledge
